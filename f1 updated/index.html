Formula 1

F1 is a single-seater auto race held predominantly in Europe. It is the highest class of single-seater racing,

featuring the fastest, most technologically advanced Formula One vehicles.

with the events typically featuring practice sessions, qualifying, and a main race. The sport has a

long history and is known for its high speeds and intense competition.



Famous Teams

1.Mercedes

The Mercedes F1 is a single-seater racing car designed by Mercedes-Benz for use in Formula One racing. Mercedes has a long history in Formula One, having won their first championship in 1954 and closing the gap between Ferrari and McLaren for the 2020 championship. The Mercedes F1 team, officially known as Mercedes-AMG Petronas Motorsport, is the works team of the German car manufacturer, and is one of the most successful in the history of the sport, winning 7 constructors' championships and 8 drivers' championships

2.Alpine

The Alpine F1 is a sports car produced by the French company Alpine. The car is typically two-seated, rear-wheel-drive, and powered by a variety of engines, including gasoline and diesel options. The Alpine F1 has a rich racing history, with several successful entries in Formula One racing. Today, the Alpine F1 is a popular choice for enthusiasts and collectors, with limited production available each year. Its sleek design and sporty performance make it a highly sought-after vehicle among collectors and car enthusiasts.

3. Hass F1 Team

The Hass F1 team is a Belize-based professional solar car team that competes in the Formula One World Championship. They have been competing in the championship since 2012. In 2018, they finished 3rd during the Constructors Championship, making them the first Belizean team to achieve such a feat. The team's success is attributed to their strong technical and strategic skills, as well as their dedicated teamwork and perseverance.

4. Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing, currently competing as Oracle Red Bull Racing and also known simply as Red Bull or RBR, Red Bull Racing is a Formula One racing team based in Salzburg, Austria. The team was founded in the early 2000s and has since seen great success in the sport, winning 11 world championships. The team is known for its unique and eye-catching branding, including the famous bull logo and the energy drink Red Bull, which shares its name.

5. MacLaren

McLaren Racing Limited is a British motor racing team based at the McLaren Technology Centre in Woking, Surrey, England. McLaren is best known as a Formula One constructor, the second oldest active team and the second most successful Formula One team after Ferrari, having won 183 races, 12 Drivers' Championships and 8 Constructors' Championships. McLaren also has a history of competing in American open wheel racing, as both an entrant and a chassis constructor, and has won the Canadian-American Challenge Cup (Can-Am) sports car racing championship.

6. Astro Martin

The Aston Martin Formula 1 team is a British racing team in Formula One motorsport. They joined the Formula 1 World Championship in 1999 and have since achieved five Grands Prix victories and several other podium finishes. The team is owned by wealthy British businessman Lawrence Stroll.A commercial rebranding of the Racing Point F1 Team resulted in the team's return as Aston Martin in 2021, utilising customer Mercedes power units. In 2026, the team will begin using Honda power units as part of a works partnership with the Japanese manufacturer.
